Fabian Spaeh

Applied Scientist at Celonis AI. I completed a PhD in computer science at Boston University where I was advised by Alina Ene. I am broadly interested in optimization, learning-augmented algorithms for online allocation problems, submodular maximization, and graph algorithms. You can contact me by email via fspaeh at bu dot edu.
